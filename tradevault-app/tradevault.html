About TradeVault

TradeVault is a comprehensive trading journal and analysis platform built for day traders and investors. It helps track trades, document strategies, store backtest results, and get AI-powered improvement suggestions — all without requiring a database.

Key Features

  • Trade journal with detailed entry/exit logging, P&L, and strategy tags
  • Strategy management with entry/exit rules and pre-trade checklists
  • Performance analytics: win rate, profit factor, Sharpe ratio, drawdown
  • Playbook for documenting A+ setups with hit rate tracking
  • Trading Jail with circuit breaker and morning check-in
  • Import from TradingView, AMP LIVE, IBKR, and CSV
  • AI-powered suggestions to identify patterns and improve

Development Note: Built entirely with Claude Code. File-based storage (JSON/CSV) means no database setup required — just run and trade.

About InvoiCRM

InvoiCRM is a production SaaS platform for field service businesses. It provides end-to-end CRM and invoicing — from lead capture to payment collection — deployed and serving real customers.

Key Features

  • Professional quote and invoice generation
  • Client relationship management
  • Job scheduling and tracking
  • Payment tracking and reminders
  • Dashboard with revenue analytics
  • Multi-tenant SaaS with 30-day free trial

Architecture

  • Next.js full-stack with server-side rendering
  • PostgreSQL with multi-tenant data isolation
  • Stripe integration for subscriptions and payments
  • Production deployment with CI/CD pipeline

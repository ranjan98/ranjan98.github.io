About Aura Companion

Aura Companion is an AI-powered companion app that offers personalized conversations with custom AI personas.

Key Features

  • Multiple AI personas to choose from (Luna, Max, Sage, Nova, Kai)
  • Voice and text chat capabilities
  • Personalized conversations based on companion personality
  • Beautiful, intuitive UI with dark mode
  • Powered by Grok AI for natural conversations

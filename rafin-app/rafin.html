About Rafin

Rafin is a production-deployed personal finance app with active users on both mobile and web. It helps users track expenses, scan receipts with OCR, and get AI-powered spending insights.

Key Features

  • Add and categorize expenses effortlessly
  • Scan receipts with OCR technology
  • Smart budget tracking and visualization
  • AI-powered spending insights and recommendations
  • Support for multiple currencies
  • Cross-platform: iOS, Android, and Web

Architecture

  • React Native mobile app deployed to App Store & Google Play
  • Next.js web app and landing page at rafin.app
  • Supabase backend with PostgreSQL, Auth, and Edge Functions
  • AI-powered OCR pipeline for receipt scanning
